FLIGHT INFORMATION

Flights depart to Fyre Cay both weekends from Nassau on the Thursday, Friday, and Saturday and return to Nassau from Fyre Cay on Saturday, Sunday or Monday.

 

You may chose any combination of flights. For example, you could leave for Fyre Cay Friday morning and return Monday morning, leave Friday evening and return Sunday evening, and so on. Flight departure times will be scheduled on a first come, first-served basis.

 

You will receive a travel preference form a few weeks after you purchase your ticket.

FLIGHT INFORMATION

Flights depart to the Bahamas both weekends from Miami on Thursday, Friday or Saturday and return to Miami from the Bahamas on Saturday, Sunday or Monday. You may choose any combination of flights. For example, you could leave for the Bahamas Friday morning and return Monday morning, or leave Friday evening and return Sunday evening. We will do our best to accommodate your preferences. Flight departure times will be scheduled on a first come, first served basis. Your ticket also includes a round-trip boat between the Bahamas and Fyre Cay. You will receive a travel preference form a few weeks after you purchase your ticket.

Boat & Jet Ski
  • Boats vary in size but are on average between 20-30 feet.
  • A full weekend is Friday-Sunday (not counting arrival day).
  • Boats can be picked up at Fyre Cay— you will receive a detailed guide prior to departure.
  • Pick-up times will be coordinated on a reservation basis. After securing a vessel, you will be sent a quick reservation form.
